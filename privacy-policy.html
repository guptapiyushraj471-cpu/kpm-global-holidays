Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and protect your personal information responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To process bookings and provide travel services

We use secure servers, encrypted technology and strict access control to protect personal information.

Privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Updated version will be posted here with an effective date.
